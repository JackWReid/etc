--- layout: work permalink: /work/moe-logic/ title: Moe Logic subtitle: Brand, graphic, and web design for a nightlife company in South West England. ---

Moe Logic is a Bath, Somerset based nightlife company that began putting on parties in 2012. At the outset, they contacted me to create for them a striking logo and brand to set them apart from the myriad other bass music nights vying for attention in the area. Landing on this striking tie dye-based logo and type motif, we were all pretty happy with the outcome.

The Moe Logic roundel started popping up around the city in the form of stickers handed out on the nights and by street teams. Meanwhile, every new night demanded a new event poster that too, was posted up around town and online. These were created by mixing brand fundamentals we established at the outset, and event-by-event modifications. What we ended up with is a wealth of cohesive but varied poster designs.
